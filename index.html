Welcome to the 2015 Congressional App Challenge!

The Congressional App Challenge, a congressional initiative to improve student engagement in coding and computer science, will run from November 9, 2015 through January 21, 2016. During the Congressional App Challenge submission period, students in participating districts can create and submit their original apps for the chance to be recognized by their Member of Congress. 2015 Congressional App Challenge Co-Chairs

Rep. Mimi Walters Rep. Hakeem Jeffries -->

Representatives Bob Goodlatte and Anna G. Eshoo selected Representatives Mimi Walters and Hakeem Jeffries to chair the 2015 Congressional App Challenge. Representatives Goodlatte and Eshoo spearheaded the creation of the Challenge and co-chaired the first Challenge in 2014. The 2015 Challenge begins on November 9 (more).

Our Sponsors

The Congressional App Challenge always welcomes sponsors who share our mission of supporting the development of technical talent in U.S. high school students. If you are interested in sponsoring the Congressional App Challenge, please contact Pamela Strother to discuss.

Our Partners

The Congressional App Challenge is actively seeking partners that share our mission to expand and strengthen STEM education in our schools. We are particularly interested in connecting with those that champion STEM education within underserved communities, and with those demographics that are traditionally underrepresented in technology. If you are interested in partnering with the Congressional App Challenge, please complete this form.
